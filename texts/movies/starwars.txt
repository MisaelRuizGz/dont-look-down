It is a dark time for the
rebellion. Although the Death Star has been destroyed, 
imperial troops have driven the rebel forces from their 
hidden base and pursued them across the galaxy. Evading 
the dreaded Imperial Starfleet, a group of freedom
fighters led by Luke Skywalker has established a new secret
base on the remote ice world of Hoth. The evil lord Darth Vader,
obsessed with finding young Skywalker, has dispatched
thousands of remote probes into the far reaches of space....
